About the Project

Rising Waters is an Artificial Intelligence based Flood Prediction System developed using Machine Learning and Flask. The system analyses weather parameters such as rainfall, humidity and cloud cover to predict whether flood conditions are likely to occur.

The application helps disaster management teams, government agencies and researchers make informed decisions by providing quick and reliable flood risk predictions.
